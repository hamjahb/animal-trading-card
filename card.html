Sea Otter

The sea otter's primary form of insulation is an exceptionally thick coat of fur, the densest in the animal kingdom.

  • Scientefic Name: Enhydra Lutris
  • Habitat: North Pacific Ocean.
  • Diet: Fish and invertebrates.
  • Weight: 14KG to 45KG

The Sea Otter is a marine mammal that is related to the Weasel Family. Living mainly in the sea, Sea otters are capable of walking on land. The sea otter use rocks as a tool to open shells.
